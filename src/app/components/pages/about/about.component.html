What is Maker Women's Challenge?


         A 6 month programme in which we are going to mentor the teams on a weekly basis to empower students, primarily girls with technical skills and to further connect them to reputed organisations to provide them with various job opportunities to make women as a leader and problem solver.

Culmination:


         Boosting the seed of innovation in the heart of the students and through this event they can learn how to work in a focused, creative and collaborative way. Believe technology will have them to be a part of the competitions in the future and it also help them to gain confidence in whatever they do.
