Challenge Terms

  • Eligible participants are School Students from age 12 - 16 Years, College Students and Housewives.
  • A 6 month programme in which we are going to mentor the teams on a weekly basis. This workshop will be performed in the form of teams. Each team consists of school students, college students, graduated women(housewives) and a qualified individual as a mentor will be allotted to them for their continuous guidance and monitoring.
  • We should brainstorm the problem statement and come up with a solution that problem with the help of technology.
  • By conducting this program we aim to build a connecting link between tier-II and tier-III cities with multinational companies to provide students in the tier-II and tier-III cities with platform experience, the culture and work ethics of these reputed companies.
